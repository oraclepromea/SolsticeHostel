About Solstice Hostel

Experience the warmth of Bolivian hospitality in the heart of Copacabana

Located in the vibrant town of Copacabana on the shores of Lake Titicaca, Solstice Hostel offers comfortable accommodations with a friendly atmosphere. Our hostel combines modern amenities with traditional Bolivian warmth, making it the perfect base for exploring this magical region.

Whether you're here to visit the famous Isla del Sol, explore local markets, or simply relax by the lake, we provide everything you need for an unforgettable stay.
